# Mora Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 20 July 2026**

Mora ("the app") is an educational market-intelligence app for iOS. This
policy explains what data the app handles, where it goes, and the choices
you have. The short version: your portfolio lives on your device, we sell
nothing, and we show no ads.

## Data stored on your device

The following never leaves your iPhone unless stated otherwise below:

- **Portfolio holdings** — the assets, quantities and buy prices you enter
  are stored locally on your device.
- **Preferences** — risk profile, tracked sectors, explanation mode,
  notification settings and similar personalisation.
- **Email address** — if you enter one during onboarding, it is stored
  locally to personalise the app. It is not transmitted to us.
- **Face ID** — the optional vault lock uses Apple's LocalAuthentication
  framework. Biometric data is processed entirely by iOS on your device;
  Mora never sees, stores or transmits it.
- **Notifications** — the morning briefing is a local notification
  scheduled on your device. No push-notification service is used.

Deleting the app deletes all of this data.

## Data processed to provide features

Two features send limited data off the device, to servers we operate or
contract, solely to produce the response shown to you:

- **AI analysis and chat.** When you use Mora's AI features, your question
  and a summary of your portfolio context (symbols, position sizes and
  current prices) are sent to our server and processed by our AI provider
  (Anthropic) to generate the analysis. This content is used only to
  produce the response. We do not use it to train models, build profiles,
  or advertise.
- **Market data.** The app requests live quotes and news for the symbols
  it displays from our market-data provider (Finnhub) via our server. These
  requests contain ticker symbols, not your identity or position sizes.

Our server sees the network metadata inherent to any internet request
(such as IP address) and standard operational logs. We do not build user
profiles from this data.

## Subscriptions and payments

Mora Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through your App
Store account. We never receive your name, payment details, or billing
address. Subscription entitlement is verified on your device using
Apple's StoreKit framework.

## What we do NOT do

- No advertising and no ad networks.
- No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
- No sale, rental or sharing of personal data with third parties for
  their own purposes.
- No cross-app or cross-site tracking. The app contains no trackers as
  defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

## Your rights

If you are in the UK, EU or another jurisdiction with data-protection
law (UK GDPR / GDPR), you have rights of access, correction, deletion
and objection. Because almost all data is stored only on your device,
you can exercise deletion at any time by removing the app or using
Sign Out (Settings → Sign out), which resets all local data.

For anything relating to server-side processing described above, contact
us and we will respond within 30 days.

## Children

Mora is not directed at children. The app deals with financial market
information and requires an App Store account.

## Changes

We will update this page when our practices change and revise the "Last
updated" date above. Material changes will be highlighted in the app's
release notes.
